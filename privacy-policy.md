# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 May 2026

Vault respects your privacy.

The app may store information you add, such as item names, photos, receipts, notes, prices, warranty details, and inventory information.

This data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, your data may also be stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple’s services.

The developer does not directly access your iCloud data.

Vault may request access to your camera, photo library, or files only so you can add photos, receipts, and documents to your inventory.

Vault does not currently use third-party advertising, tracking, or data brokers.

You can delete your data inside the app. Deleting the app may remove locally stored data. If iCloud sync is enabled, some data may remain in iCloud until removed there.

This policy may be updated from time to time.
